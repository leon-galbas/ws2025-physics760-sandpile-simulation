Compared to the zero initialization, both schemes reach the stationary regime significantly faster. The scaling exponents $a$ for the PDFs are obtained from the slope $m\pm\Delta m$ of the plots via
\begin{equation}
  a = 1-m, \qquad \delta a = \delta m.
\end{equation}
The scaling exponents for the conditional expectation values are directly obtained from $m$ and $\Delta m$. Table \ref{tab:scaling-exponents} gives an overview over all computed scaling exponents for all sandpile configurations. Additional plots for individual fits can be found in the appendix.
